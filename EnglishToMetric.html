English To Metric

This program converts english measurements into metric measurements. it also convert metric measurements into english measurement. In order to create this program I had figure out how to convert english measuremnt such as inches, mile, fee, and yards into metric meaurements. What another layer of difficulty is that the user should be able to enter all of the measurements at once and convert it into their desired which ever form of measurement they choose. essential I had to figure out how to convert four measurements into three, if going from english to metric. And convert three metric measurements into four english measurement when going from metric to english.
